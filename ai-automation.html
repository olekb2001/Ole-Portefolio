AI Lead Automation

This page demonstrates a full AI-powered automation workflow built using HTML, CSS, Make.com, Google Sheets, and OpenAI. When you submit the form below, the backend automatically stores your message, generates an AI summary, and updates a structured CRM sheet.

Project Overview

This project simulates a real-world lead management system. It captures user messages, organizes them in a CRM, and uses AI to instantly understand the intent and suggest next actions. This demonstrates how automation and AI can reduce manual workload for businesses.

How It Works

  • User submits the form below.
  • A Make.com webhook receives the data.
  • Make creates a new row in Google Sheets.
  • OpenAI generates a summary and identifies intent.
  • The sheet row is updated with AI insights.
